4-Phase Adaptive Cooling Logic

  • 1 TURBO — 30W (100%) — Full Peltier power. Rapid cool-down from ambient to 10°C target.
  • 2 OFF — 0W — Module switches off once target is reached. PCM buffer absorbs residual heat.
  • 3 ECO — 9W (30%) — Maintenance mode. Holds 10°C throughout the session with minimal power draw.
  • 4 CYCLE — Automatic 2↔3 loop. Intelligently alternates OFF and ECO until the bottle is refilled.

About VERBION V2.5

VERBION V2.5 replaces passive insulation with active thermoelectric cooling. The Bi₂Te₃ Peltier module pumps heat out of your drink at up to 30W, achieving the 10°C sports-hydration sweet spot in under 40 minutes from 25°C tap water.

Once target temperature is reached, the system transitions automatically to 9W ECO cycling — maintaining 10°C for your entire session without draining the battery. A Bio-Wax PCM thermal buffer in the cap reduces heat leak by 8× compared to a standard cap, acting as a latent-heat reservoir between duty cycles.

The OLED display shows real-time temperature, current cooling mode, and battery percentage. Haptic vibration alerts notify you when your target temperature is reached. At ≤28 dBA, the dual fans are quieter than a library whisper.
